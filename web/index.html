Happy Thanksgiving

I love Thanksgiving, it tops the list of my favorite holidays. I love being with my family, sitting around a dinner table, feeling thankful and loved. I also love cooking, spreadsheets, planning and pie. Just my luck, Thanksgiving manages to seamlesssly incorporate all of these things.

This was my third year hosting Thanksgiving and I wanted to make it a challenge. So, I invited 22 people and asked or begged them not to bring anything but wine (because, seriously. You can never have too much wine), and got to work preparing a feast. The goal was to plan it so well, that I would not freak out. Not even once, and come 6:00 when the guests arrive, I will take off my apron, pour myself a glass of wine and mingle while the turkey rests. This may or may not have happened... read on!
